Jewelry Care

How to clean my jewelry?

Wipe gently with a soft dry cloth. For anti-tarnish, rinse under water & pat dry. Avoid chemicals, toothpaste & sanitizer.

Best way to store jewelry?

Keep each piece separately in its pouch or a zip-lock bag. Away from sunlight & moisture. Don't pile pieces together — they'll scratch.

Can I wear it in the shower or gym?

Anti-tarnish → Yes! shower, gym, rain — no problem.
Handmade → Remove first water damages plating.

Any golden rule for jewelry?

"Last thing on, first thing off."

Apply perfume, lotion & spray first → let dry → then wear jewelry.
