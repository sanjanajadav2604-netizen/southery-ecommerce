Jewelry Care Guide

Every piece from Southery Sentie is crafted to last. With the right care, your jewelry will stay as beautiful as the day you received it.

💧

Avoid Water

Remove before swimming, bathing, or washing hands

🧴

No Perfume

Apply perfume & lotion before putting on jewelry

📦

Store Safely

Keep in the pouch provided, away from sunlight

🧪

No Chemicals

Keep away from cleaning products & harsh chemicals

🛡️

Anti-Tarnish Jewelry Care

PVD Coated — Built to Last

Our Anti-Tarnish pieces use an advanced PVD (Physical Vapour Deposition) coating process that bonds a gold layer onto a stainless steel base. This makes them far more durable than regular gold-plated jewelry — but they still need basic care.

✅ Do This

✓

Wipe gently with a soft dry cloth after each wear

✓

Store each piece separately in its pouch

✓

Put jewelry on last — after makeup and perfume

✓

Clean with a soft damp cloth if needed, then dry immediately

❌ Avoid This

✖

Wearing in the shower, pool, or sea

✖

Contact with perfume, deodorant, or body lotion directly

✖

Using abrasive cloths or chemical cleaners

✖

Storing in humid bathrooms or direct sunlight

💡 Good to know

Anti-tarnish coating is not the same as "waterproof." While our pieces resist tarnishing much better than regular plated jewelry, prolonged water or chemical exposure will still degrade the coating over time. Basic care goes a long way.

🤲

Handmade Jewelry Care

Crafted by Hand — Unique by Nature

Each handmade piece is one-of-a-kind. Slight variations in color, texture, or shape are natural — they are marks of authentic craftsmanship, not defects. Handmade pieces often use threads, beads, stones, and mixed materials that need extra care.

✅ Do This

✓

Store flat in the box or soft pouch to protect shape

✓

Handle gently — avoid pulling on delicate threads or wires

✓

Keep away from sharp surfaces that could scratch stones

✓

Dust lightly with a soft brush if needed

❌ Avoid This

✖

Washing or submerging in water

✖

Wearing during heavy physical activity or sports

✖

Tangling necklaces with other pieces

✖

Exposing beaded or threaded pieces to direct sunlight for long

How to Clean Your Jewelry

A simple 3-step cleaning routine that works for both collections.

1

Dry wipe after every wear

Use a clean, soft microfibre cloth to gently wipe off sweat, oils, and dust after each use. This alone prevents most tarnishing.

2

Light damp clean (monthly)

Dampen a soft cloth slightly with plain water. Wipe the jewelry gently. Immediately dry with another cloth — never leave moisture on the surface.

3

Store properly after cleaning

Place back in the Southery Sentie pouch or box. Keep pieces separate so they don't scratch each other. Store in a cool, dry place.

⚠️ Never use these on your jewelry

Toothpaste, baking soda, lemon juice, vinegar, ultrasonic cleaners, or any commercial jewelry cleaner. These can strip the coating on anti-tarnish pieces and damage handmade materials.

Storage Tips

👍

Use the pouch

Every Southery Sentie order comes with a protective pouch. Always store your piece in it when not wearing.

💿

Store separately

Don't let pieces touch each other in storage — they scratch. Use individual pouches or small zip-lock bags.

🚿

Not in the bathroom

Bathroom humidity accelerates tarnishing. Store in a bedroom drawer or jewelry box instead.
